Quick Rules Reference

Keep the game moving — answers at your fingertips

Combat Order

1. SurpriseAmbushers vs. Perception. Surprised creatures skip first turn, can't react.
2. InitiativeRoll d20 + DEX modifier. Ties: higher DEX goes first. Re-roll each encounter.
3. Turn1 Action + 1 Bonus Action + 1 Reaction + Movement (up to Speed).
4. Round6 seconds in-game. Everyone acts once per round.

Attack Rolls

Hitd20 + ability mod + proficiency (if proficient) ≥ target AC.
Crit (20)Natural 20 always hits. Roll damage dice twice, add mods once.
Fumble (1)Natural 1 always misses.
CoverHalf: +2 AC/DEX. Three-quarters: +5 AC/DEX. Full: can't be targeted.
Adv/DisRoll 2d20, take highest (Advantage) or lowest (Disadvantage). Cancel out.

Movement

Difficult TerrainCosts double movement. No stacking.
JumpingLong: STR score in feet (running), half (standing). High: 3 + STR mod ft.
ProneCosts half speed to stand. Melee attacks have Adv, ranged have Dis.
GrappleAttack with Athletics vs. target's Athletics or Acrobatics. Speed → 0.
ShoveAthletics vs. Athletics/Acrobatics. Knock prone or push 5 ft.

Spellcasting in Combat

ConcentrationTake damage → CON save DC = max(10, half damage). On fail: spell ends.
Opportunity AttackTriggered when a creature leaves your reach. Uses Reaction. Melee only.
Casting + BonusCast bonus action spell → only cantrips as main action that turn.
CounterspellReaction. Automatically stops ≤3rd level. Higher: Ability check DC = 10 + spell level.

Status Conditions

Blinded
Can't see. Auto-fail sight checks. Attack rolls have Disadv; attacks against have Adv.
Charmed
Can't attack charmer. Charmer has Adv on social checks vs. creature.
Deafened
Can't hear. Auto-fail hearing checks.
Exhaustion
1: Disadv on checks. 2: Speed halved. 3: Disadv attacks & saves. 4: HP max halved. 5: Speed 0. 6: Death.
Frightened
Disadv on checks & attacks while source is in sight. Can't willingly move closer.
Grappled
Speed = 0. Ends if grappler incapacitated or moved out of reach.
Incapacitated
No actions or reactions.
Invisible
Can't be seen. Attack rolls have Adv; attacks against have Disadv.
Paralyzed
Incapacitated, can't move or speak. Auto-fail STR/DEX saves. Attacks against have Adv. Hits within 5 ft. = crit.
Petrified
Transformed to stone. Incapacitated, can't move/speak. Resistant to all damage. Immune to poison/disease.
Poisoned
Disadv on attack rolls and ability checks.
Prone
Melee attacks have Adv against it; ranged Disadv. Must spend half speed to stand.
Restrained
Speed 0. Attack rolls have Disadv. Attacks against have Adv. Disadv on DEX saves.
Stunned
Incapacitated, can't move, only speak falteringly. Auto-fail STR/DEX saves. Attacks against have Adv.
Unconscious
Incapacitated, drops items/falls prone. Auto-fail STR/DEX saves. Attacks have Adv. Hits in 5 ft. = crit.

Standard Actions

AttackOne attack (or more with Extra Attack). Includes unarmed strikes.
Cast a SpellCast a spell with casting time of 1 action.
DashDouble your movement speed this turn.
DisengageMovement doesn't provoke opportunity attacks for rest of turn.
DodgeAttacks against you have Disadv (if you can see attacker). Adv on DEX saves.
HelpGive Adv to an ally's next ability check or attack roll vs. a creature you can see.
HideStealth check. Must be obscured. Success: hidden until you reveal yourself or fail a check.
ReadyDeclare trigger + reaction. Trigger fires your action + reaction before next turn.
SearchPerception or Investigation check to find something.
Use ObjectInteract with a second object (first interaction is free).

Bonus Actions

Off-hand AttackTwo-weapon fighting with light weapons. No ability mod to damage unless negative.
Class FeaturesCunning Action (Rogue), Flurry of Blows (Monk), Wild Shape (Druid), etc.
Bonus SpellsSpells with casting time "1 bonus action".

Reactions

Opportunity AttackWhen creature you can see leaves your reach. One melee attack.
CounterspellInterrupt a spell being cast within 60 ft. (Wizard/Sorcerer/Warlock).
Shield+5 AC until start of next turn (Wizard spell, uses slot).
Readied ActionFire your readied action when the trigger occurs.

Death Saving Throws

Each TurnRoll d20 (no modifiers). 10+ = success. Under 10 = failure.
3 SuccessesStabilize at 0 HP. Unconscious but no longer dying.
3 FailuresDeath. Failures don't need to be consecutive.
Natural 20Regain 1 HP. Conscious and upright.
Natural 1Counts as two failures.
Damage at 0One failure. If damage ≥ HP max, instant death. If crit, two failures.
HealingAny healing at 0 HP makes creature conscious with that many HP. Resets death saves.

Resting

Short Rest1+ hour. Spend any number of Hit Dice to heal (roll + CON mod). Regain some class features.
Long Rest8 hours. Regain all HP and half max Hit Dice (min 1). Regain all spell slots, most class features. Max 1 per 24 hours.
Interrupted Rest1+ hour combat/spellcasting/hard travel interrupts and must restart.

Healing

Healer's KitAction, expend use: stabilize creature at 0 HP without a check.
Medicine CheckDC 10 Wisdom (Medicine) to stabilize a dying creature.
Massive DamageIf damage ≥ your HP max in one hit: CON save DC 15 or drop to 0 HP.
